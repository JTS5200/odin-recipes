Chilli Mix

Ingredients

  • 500g Minced Beef
  • 400g (1 Can) Tomatoes
  • 400g (1 Can) Kidney Beans
  • 1 Large Onion
  • 1 Red Onion
  • 2 Carrots
  • 2 Sticks of Celery
  • 4 Cloves of garlic
  • 1 Beef or Veg stock cube
  • 2 Tablespoons of Tomato Puree
  • 3 Tablespoons of Taco Seasoning
  • Salt and Pepper to Taste

Steps

  1. Dice the onions, carrots and celery, and cook for 5 minutes with oil of choice
  2. Cook the meat in pan until browned. Drain Excess.
  3. Combine meat with softened vegtable mix, add minced garlic and taco seasoning. Continue to cook for 2 more minutes while giving a good mixeroni.
  4. Drain and wash kidney beans. Mash half and add to main mixture, keep other half unmashed for later.
  5. Add tomato puree, can of Tomatoes, 1 vegtable stock cube, salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Mix well and let simmer for 20 minutes. This is important to let those flavours get together and partayyyy.
  7. Add the rest of the Kidney beans and stir through, cook for another 5 minutes.
  8. Voila! Dinner is served.
