Air Fried Nandos Wings

Ingredients

  • 14 Wings (Typically 28 in a pack)
  • 3-4 Tablespoons of the Nandoes Sauce Of Choice
  • 2 Tablespoons of Flour
  • 3 Teaspoons of Piri-Piri seasoning

Steps

  1. Cover the wings in the Piri-Piri seasoning, toss around to ensure good coating coverage.
  2. Cover wings with flour, toss to ensure even coating.
  3. Air Fryer at 200 Degrees. Cook wings for 18 minutes total, turning over after 10 mins of cooking.
  4. Place wings in bowl and coat with the Nandoes sauce. Throw around to ensure even coating.
  5. Enjoy
